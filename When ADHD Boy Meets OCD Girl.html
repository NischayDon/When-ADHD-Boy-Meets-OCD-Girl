When ADHD Boy Meets OCD Girl

He lives in moments, she lives by minutes.

He thrives in improvisation, she finds peace in precision.

He forgets, she double-checks.

He runs late, she plans ahead.

He feels everything all at once;

She feels too much, but hides it neatly in symmetry.

And yet—

When they meet, it’s not chaos meeting order.

It’s freedom meeting fear.

It’s color meeting canvas.

It’s the wild rhythm finding its metronome.

He’s thunder; she’s the steady rain,

He breaks the pattern, she mends the chain.

He speaks in sparks, she writes in grace —

Two restless hearts, one quiet place.

And when the world forgets their tune,

They dance beneath a fragile moon.

For love is not to fix or mend,

But where differences find their blend.
